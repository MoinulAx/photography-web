About Me

I am deeply passionate about web and Full Stack Development, but my creative pursuits extend beyond coding. As a photographer and musician, I express my artistic vision through captivating visuals and soulful music. I operate Rummy Shoots, a contracting company where I specialize in video editing and photography. My goal is to create compelling visuals that resonate with audiences and tell meaningful stories. As an experienced editor, I bring a keen eye for detail and a wealth of technical expertise to each project, ensuring high-quality results that exceed expectations. I have my own studio, which allows me to offer a range of media services tailored to your needs. I am always eager to connect with new individuals and explore opportunities for collaboration. If you appreciate my portfolio and are interested in my media services, please don’t hesitate to reach out. I would love to connect and discuss how we can create something great together!
